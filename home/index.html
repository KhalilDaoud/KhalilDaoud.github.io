Carpe Diem Poetry

The "Carpe Diem" themed works of Andrew Marvell and Robert Herrick.


"Carpe Diem" is a Latin phrase that translates as "seize the day", or, make the most out of the time we have, get up and do something, or, don't let life pass you by. Carpe Diem is used by many authors to help convince the reader or, person to the work was addressed to, to go and do something while they still can. Here we focus on Andrew marvell and Robert Herrick's use of Carpe Diem in their seduction poetry. Carpe Diem was used by both authors to convince their lovers to not be so frugal, and to enjoy their youth while they still can.
